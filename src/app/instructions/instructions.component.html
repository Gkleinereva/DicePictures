Welcome to the dice picture generator!

Do you want a portrait made out of dice but are too lazy to actually make it? Then we have the solution for you.

Instructions

Step 1: Upload the picture you want "dicified".

Step 2: Configure the options to determine how you want your picture dicified.

Step 3: See what happens, and tweek the picture and settings until it's perfect!

Pro tips discovered through a lot of trial and error

  • High resolution images work better and give you more options. Try to use an image with dimensions of at least 500 pixels by 500 pixels.
  • The subject should take up the majority of the image (i.e. small backgrounds are best).
  • For portraits, blank backgrounds work best.
